FAQ Question

How long does it take for an organ to be transported from one hospital to another?

Approximately 45 minutes to an hour, depending on the distance between locations.

What are the technologies used to maintain organ vitals during transportation?

To maintain organ vitals during transportation, we utilize high-tech sensors and advanced technologies. Through our website we monitor and control the temperature and pressure levels, ensuring maximum safety and preservation of the organs throughout the flight.

Can you provide real-time updates on organ transportation?

Yes, our platform offers real-time tracking and monitoring. You can receive timely updates on the status, location, and condition of the transported organs, ensuring transparency and peace of mind throughout the process.

How do you handle emergencies during organ transportation?

Our drones are equipped with a "back-to-home" feature that ensures safety during emergencies. In the event of a lost connection or unforeseen circumstances, the drone automatically returns to its starting location, safeguarding the organs being transported.
